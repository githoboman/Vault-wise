# BitCredit: The Vision

## Bridging Bitcoin Liquidity to Global Credit
BitCredit is a decentralized protocol designed to merge the security and liquidity of **Bitcoin** with the specialized credit infrastructure of the **Creditcoin (CTC)** ecosystem. We believe that Bitcoin holders shouldn't have to sell their assets to participate in modern financial markets; instead, they should be able to use their holdings as the ultimate collateral for trustless, transparent credit.

## Key Pillars

### 1. Bitcoin as the Ultimate Collateral
By leveraging **Stacks (sBTC)**, BitCredit allows users to lock Bitcoin in a non-custodial vault. This locked value is then "teleported" to the Creditcoin EVM as **Credit Power**, turning a static asset into a dynamic engine for borrowing.

### 2. Deterministic, Trustless Credit
Unlike traditional credit which relies on opaque centralized scoring, BitCredit credit lines are deterministic. If the collateral is in the vault, the credit is issued. No middleman, no bias—just code and math.

### 3. Cross-Chain Synergy
BitCredit is a pioneer in the "Three-Layer" architecture:
- **Layer 1 (Bitcoin)**: The source of truth and ultimate settlement.
- **Layer 2 (Stacks)**: The programmable logic layer for Bitcoin collateral.
- **Layer 3 (Creditcoin)**: The specialized EVM for credit records, loan lifecycle management, and interest settlement.

### 4. Expanding the CTC Ecosystem
BitCredit isn't just a bridge; it's an accelerator for Creditcoin. By bringing Bitcoin's $1T+ market cap to the USC (Universal Social Credit) contracts, we significantly increase transaction volume, TVL, and real-world utility for the CTC network.

## The Roadmap
- **Phase I**: Trustless Collateral Vaults (Stacks) & Credit Line Issuance (Creditcoin).
- **Phase II**: Decentralized Lending Pools & Interest bearing USC.
- **Phase III**: Real-world integration and BTC-backed business credit.
